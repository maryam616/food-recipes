Chicken Biryani Recipe

Home Add Recipe Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams of chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, 2 tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste, spices, oil, mint, and coriander

Instructions

  1. Soak rice, boil until 50% done.
  2. Fry onions, add chicken, yogurt, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Layer rice and chicken, sprinkle herbs, and cook on low heat.
  4. now its ready and you can Serve it!!!
